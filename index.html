Bio

Hi, I’m Mac and I’m a frontend web developer.

I recently decided to end my first opportunity in this industry as a professional frontend web developer. I'm grateful for the opportunity that company gave me to kickstart my career but I want to focus my efforts on different, more prevalent technologies like Angular and Express. I thrive on creating something from nothing and given my newfound set of skills in the realm of programming, I can’t wait to join a team that will benefit from my unique skill set and perspective.

I embrace challenges in and outside the world of web development including: snowboarding, woodworking, guitar, and pottery. There’s nothing more satisfying than overcoming a difficult task to produce something beautiful and functional. That said, I’m excited to embark on this new path of continual learning and creating!
